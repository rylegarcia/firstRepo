Danyl is a Fashion Editor/Stylist based in between Toronto and New York. He currently serves as Editor-in-Chief/Creative Director of TOM* magazine–Toronto men’s fashion magazine, published and produced by Toronto Men’s Fashion Week Inc., and also works for Condé Nast in the Fashion and Art Department where he assists in fashion sittings and write reviews to menswear shows for WWD.

With over 5 years of experience in the industry, Danyl has contributed to various publications such as Vman, Numero Homme, Schon–to mention a few. He is a regular attendee to men’s shows in Milan, Paris and New York and has covered major shows including Louis Vuitton, Vivienne Westwood, Dsquared, Roberto Cavalli, Valentino, Raf Simons, Jil Sander and many more. In his role as a Fashion Editor, he has worked and interviewed designers and fashion icons such as Phillip Lim, Alexander Wang, Nick Wooster, Public School and styled relevant models of today.
